Next v0.1.X
-----------

Cultures:

- Added namelist for Vandal culture
- Added dynasty names for Roman, Romano-Gallic, Romano-Hispanic, Romano-Thracian, Romano-Illyrian and Romano-Aramean cultures
- Added many new name equivalencies and fixed some existing ones

Localization:
- Added numerous cultural title localization for Britannia, Gallia, Italia, Hispania, Africa, Eastern Roman Empire, Persia, Pontic-Caspian steppe, Tamazgha and Germania Magna
- Fixed some typos among the title names

History:
- Expanded Nepos' Dalmatia based on a source, which states that it included lands between Drina and Sava. In addition, Dalmatia is now controls Epidaurum instead of ERE
- Some work on character cultures:
   - Nepos and Glycerius were made Romano-Illyrian because they were natives to Dalmatia
   - All the popes are now have appropriate cultures to reflect their origins
   - Macrinus, Aemilian and some Roman families were made Romano-African
- Made Burdigala Romano-Gallic
- Expanded Syagrius' Gaul to include Reistete and Viromandia. This made because all the maps show that Syagrius' kingdom controlled some territories north of Durocorturum
- Added provincial governors for Asia, Lycia, Lydia, Achaea and Olisipo
- Roman family trees were completely reworked. The new families include Tullii Cicerones, Aelii Tuberones, Cassii Longini and Cassii of Nicaea, patrician Aemilii, Marii Maximi/Perpetui, Marii Venantii Severini, Julii Aspri, Stertinii, Fonteii, Pomponii Bassi and family of T. Pomponius Atticus, family of Tacitus and Julius Agricola, Sallustii, Faltonii, Betitii, Virii, Servillii, Fabii Maximi and Fabii Titiani, Aquilii, Metilii, Eggii, Caecilii Metelli, Musonii, Junii Silani, Junii Maximi and Junii Rufini, Caesonii, Cornelii Scipiones, Munatii, Caesennii, Ragonii, Turranii, Scribonii, Octavii Laenates, Rubelii, Tineii, Asinii Polliones, Pompeii Magni and Pompeii of Mytilene, Arrii, Seii, Clodii Celsini, Porcii, Livii, Terentii, Hedii Lolliani, Ummidii, Marcii, family of Fl. Sabinus Antiochus Damonicus, families of Valerian and Trebonianus Gallus, Consentii, Decimii, Pontii, Plotii, Synesii and Triarii. The greatly reworked ones are Anicii, Petronii, Rufii, Claudii Marcelli, Neratii, Sulpicii Galbae, Cornelii Lentuli and Cornelii Sullae, Egnatii, Maecii, Memmii of Gigthis, Fulvii of Leptis, Caelii, Cocceii, Turcii and Acilii Glabriones. 
- All the Roman character now have names consisting of only one word to match the namelists. Also, some names were simply too long, breaking the localization.
- Filed up title history of the Senate, Urban prefecture, Aegyptus, Syria, Arabia, Cappadocia, Galatia, Cilicia, Lycia, Asia, Thracia, Macedonia, Germania Superior, Germania Inferior, Baetica, Lusitania, Africa, Dalmatia, Pannonia, Sardinia, Sicilia, Bithynia, Achaia, Cyrenaica and Creta with the Roman characters.
- Added ancient Athenian nobility starting with the 6th century BC. The major figures are Solon, Plato, Pisistratus, Miltiades, Cimon, Themistocles, Pericles and Conon. In 476 bookmark Athens are now ruled by archon Theagenes, who was descendant of these families.

- Fixed duplicate Creones/Western Isles duchy

- Liege's may demand settled vassals convert (if the liege has State Religion)
