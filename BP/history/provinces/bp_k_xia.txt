# Notes:
# Gushi fill Indo-Aryan role on this map:
# https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/3/38/Nirun%2C_Tuyuhun%2C_Yueban%2C_Tuoba_Wei.jpg
#
# Tanguts helped suppress a Sogdian revolt in 721 in Ordos City (east of Wuluhai), so that region was probably
# still Indo-Aryan (or in this case, Tocharian)
#
# https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_the_Tanguts#720s
#
# The Buddhists sects the Indo-Aryans introduced to China were likely older than the dharmaguptaka
#
# https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sutra_of_Forty-two_Chapters : Yeuzhi monks transmit Buddhism, noted sutras older than dharmaguptaka
#
# There was some presence of Qiang/Tangut people in the Hexi Corridor before being displaced by Xiongnu
# https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hexi_Corridor#Qin_dynasty
#
# Tangut remigration back to the Hexi corridor (after Tibetan pressure expelled them) didn't occur until the 900s
# Other than that, used some Vanilla CK3 references to work out Tuyuhun and Xiongnu provinces
#
# other sources:
# https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tangut_people
